History of the Android's Mascot

Probably you know that android operational system, kept by Google, is one of the most utilized for mobile devices in the world. But might you not know that your friendly mascot has a name and a curius history? Follow me on this article to learn a lot of things about this little robot.

The First Version

The first try to create a mascto came out in 2007 and came from a developer called Dan Morill . He said that opened the linkscape (free software for designs) and created his own vertion of the rebot. The objective was personalize the system only for his staff, there was no existed any order from the company to create a mascot.

That first weird robot even was named in tribute at your own creator. It will be the Dandroids.

Came Out a New Mascot

The ideia of having a mascot was becoming ripen and the mission was got to a professional design. The russian ilustrator Irina Blok , also worker from Google, she got the mission of represent the little robot in a way more friendly.

The main Irina's idea was representin everything graphically wtih little trace and in a way more plain. The drawing also should generade a quickly identification with who look at. So, came out the Bugdroid, the new mascot from Android.

The main inspiration for the trace of the new mascot Bugdroid came from those little doll that illustrated resthroom's doors to indicate the gender of each door. Tells the legend that the artist was creating in her desk in Google's office and look at the side of the bathrooms and the identification was immeditely: simple, clean, objective.
